This application is designed to assist and guide every client in the construction industry. It aims to enhance communication between engineers and clients, improving distant interactions. The application provides clients with detailed information about the company, the status of their projects, financial details, and much more. With this app, clients and engineers no longer need to meet in person or travel to the construction site. They can continue their work without worrying about travel or time constraints. Clients can use the application to access all the details about their projects and monitor the progress of their work globally. Users can access project details and communicate with the construction team from anywhere in the world. The details are available below.
